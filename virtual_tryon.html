Virtual Try-on

Virtual Try-On (VTO) is an AI and AR-driven technology that allows customers to visualize apparel, accessories, or cosmetics on themselves instantly, reducing returns and increasing purchase confidence. Using 3D mapping and computer vision, it enables virtual fitting for clothing, cosmetics, and eyewear, fostering a more engaging and personalized online shopping experience.
